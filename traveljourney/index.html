How it works.

SEEON is built for professional relevance in shared physical contexts, without turning people into dots on a map.

01 | Keyword matching

Offer, seek and interest.

People describe what they offer, what they seek and what they care about. SEEON matches these signals into relevant professional possibilities. Offer: Specific Solution · Seek: Exchange · Interest: New Distributor.

02 | Opt-in visibility

You decide when you are findable.

Visibility is intentional. You choose when your profile can appear to relevant people in the same business context.

03 | A list, not a map

Ranked by relevance.

SEEON shows a relevance-ranked contact list. No map, no real-time location and no location data.

Why not just LinkedIn?

Both can support professional networking. They solve different moments.

LinkedIn

  • Shows your broad professional network.
  • Works before and after an event.
  • Often depends on search, posts or existing connections.

SEEON

  • Shows who is relevant now, in the same room or business context.
  • Uses opt-in keyword matching, not scraping.
  • No map, no location data and GDPR-compliant by design.

How it appears.

SEEON moves with real business contexts, recognizes proximity and relevance in the moment, and guides people toward conversations that would otherwise have remained invisible.

01 | Physical context

The room is already full of relevance.

At an event or conference, many people share interests, timing and intent. SEEON treats this existing proximity as the starting point, not as coincidence.

02 | Interface

The product stays calm and clear.

The interface focuses on orientation, not distraction. Relevance becomes readable in a simple mobile moment.

03 | Outcome

Signal becomes encounter.

The goal is not a feed, but a high-quality conversation. SEEON helps relevant people avoid missing each other.

04 | Journey

Professional proximity travels with you.

Relevance can appear across the whole journey: during an event, in the hotel lobby, at the airport, at the station or on the train. SEEON is designed for these moments of shared professional context, wherever they naturally happen.
